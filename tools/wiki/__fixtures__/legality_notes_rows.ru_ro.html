Small quantities punishable by a large fine for first offenders or 6 months to 2 years in prison if the person has been convicted before. Possession of large amounts or trafficking is punishable by 2-7 years of jail time. [219] Decriminalization proposed. [220] Limited medical use approved in 2013. [221]
Russia Illegal Illegal
Main article: Cannabis in Russia
Possession of up to {{cvt|6|g|frac=5}} of cannabis (or {{cvt|2|g|frac=20|disp=comma}} of hashish) is an administrative offense, punishable by a fine of 5,000 rubles or detention of up to 15 days. Possession of larger amounts is a criminal offense.
Australia Illegal Legal
Main article: Cannabis in Australia
Australian Capital Territory legalized possession of up to 50 g and home cultivation of two plants on 31 January 2020. Other states maintain criminal penalties for recreational use, while medical access varies by jurisdiction and product authorization. Civil penalties can apply for small quantities outside the ACT.
